Admissions Open

Join Our School Family

Enrolment is open for all classes from Play Group to Std 12th and Junior College. Follow these simple steps to secure your child's place at Rising Angels.

01

Get in Touch

Email us or walk in to our school on Walgaon Road during school hours — we're open from 8 AM on all working days.

02

School Visit & Counselling

Schedule a visit to tour the school, meet our staff, and get personalised guidance for your child's class.

03

Submit Documents

Bring the birth certificate, previous school marksheets, and passport-size photographs to complete your application.

04

Welcome to Rising Angels!

Complete fee payment (cash accepted) and receive your admission confirmation. Your child's journey begins here.
